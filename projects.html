GAME DESIGN --

Springs with Guns (Working Title)

| Status: In Development ▶️

Tools: Godot Engine.

Currently in the prototyping phase. An attempt to create a reusable fighting game template, then produce a casual fighting game out of it with an emphasis on simple controls and a reliance on the interaction between spring physics and gun recoil for movement.

Current progress is updated on my twitter feed quite sporadically.
